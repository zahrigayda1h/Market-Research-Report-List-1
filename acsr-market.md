<p><strong>The ACSR Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>ACSR</li><li>ACSR/AW</li><li>ACSR/TW</li></ul></p>
<p><p>ACSR (Aluminium Conductor Steel Reinforced) is a widely used conductor type in overhead power lines, characterized by its lightweight and strength. ACSR/AW (Aluminium Conductor Steel Reinforced/Alumoweld) features an aluminum-coated steel core for added corrosion resistance. ACSR/TW (Aluminium Conductor Steel Reinforced/Trapezoidal Wire) employs trapezoidal-shaped aluminum wires, enhancing conductivity and reducing wind loading. Each type serves specific environmental and structural needs, catering to various applications in power distribution while balancing conductivity, strength, and durability.</p></p>
