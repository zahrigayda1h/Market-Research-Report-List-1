<p><p>The medical respiratory mask market comprises various types, including Positive Pressure Air Respirators and Long Tube Respirators. Positive Pressure Air Respirators deliver a continuous flow of air, ensuring that the breathing air is uncontaminated, ideal for healthcare settings with high infection risk. Long Tube Respirators, on the other hand, connect to a centralized air supply and provide more extended breathing support, commonly used in critical care units. Both types enhance patient safety and comfort while minimizing exposure to airborne contaminants.</p></p>
