<p><p>The apple juice market is primarily divided into two main types: cloudy and transparent apple juice. Cloudy apple juice, often referred to as "fresh-pressed" or "unfiltered," retains pulp and natural sediments, providing a more robust flavor and nutrient profile. It appeals to health-conscious consumers seeking natural products. In contrast, transparent apple juice undergoes filtration, offering a clear appearance and longer shelf life, catering to those preferring a refined taste. Both types target different consumer preferences, driving market diversity.</p></p>
