<p><strong>The Composite Tube Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Round Tubes</li><li>Profile Tubes</li><li>Conical Tubes</li><li>Pre-Preg Tubes</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The composite tube market comprises various types designed for specific applications. Round tubes are widely used for structural purposes, offering high strength and lightweight properties. Profile tubes feature non-circular shapes, ideal for customized designs in aerospace and automotive sectors. Conical tubes taper at one end, utilized in applications like exhaust systems or specialized machinery. Pre-preg tubes are manufactured using pre-impregnated fibers, ensuring consistent quality and improved performance, often applied in high-stress environments such as sports equipment and high-performance vehicles.</p></p>
