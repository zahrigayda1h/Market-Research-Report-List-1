<p><p>The Micromobility Telematics System market can be categorized into three main types: embedded, tethered, and integrated systems. Embedded systems are built directly into the micromobility vehicles, offering seamless data collection and communication. Tethered systems use external devices, such as smartphones or other hardware, to gather and transmit data about vehicle usage. Integrated systems combine both embedded and tethered technologies, providing enhanced functionality and user experience by allowing for more comprehensive data analysis and connectivity across various platforms.</p></p>
