<p><p>Passive battery cell balancing is a technique used to manage the charge levels of individual cells within a battery pack. This method involves dissipating excess energy from more charged cells as heat, ensuring that all cells reach similar voltage levels, thereby enhancing the overall performance and lifespan of the battery. This approach is particularly prevalent in lithium-ion batteries, common in electric vehicles and renewable energy storage systems.</p><p>The Passive Battery Cell Balancing Market is experiencing significant growth, driven by the rising demand for efficient energy storage solutions, especially with the expansion of electric vehicles and renewable energy sources.
