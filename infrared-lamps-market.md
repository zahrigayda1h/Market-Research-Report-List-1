<p><ul><li>Near Infrared</li><li>Medium Infrared</li><li>Far Infrared</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The infrared lamps market is categorized into three main types based on wavelength: near infrared, medium infrared, and far infrared. Near infrared lamps emit wavelengths between 0.75 to 1.5 micrometers and are commonly used for therapeutic heating and drying processes. Medium infrared lamps, ranging from 1.5 to 5 micrometers, find applications in industrial heating and food processing. Far infrared lamps, with wavelengths over 5 micrometers, are often utilized in heating solutions and wellness products, promoting deep tissue penetration and comfort.</p></p>
