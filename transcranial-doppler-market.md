<p><strong>The Transcranial Doppler Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>M-mode Display</li><li>B-mode Display</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Transcranial Doppler market is categorized primarily into M-mode and B-mode displays. M-mode (motion mode) provides real-time visualization of blood flow velocities, allowing for detailed analysis of dynamic movement patterns within cerebral vessels. In contrast, B-mode (brightness mode) offers a two-dimensional grayscale image that helps in visualizing anatomical structures, enhancing the understanding of cerebral blood flow and vessel identification. These display types serve distinct diagnostic purposes, catering to different clinical and research needs in neurovascular assessment.</p></p>
