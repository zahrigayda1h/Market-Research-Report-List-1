<p><ul><li>Air Pressure Bottle</li><li>Hand Pressing Bottle</li><li>Bucket</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The foam cleaner market comprises three primary types: air pressure bottles, hand-pressing bottles, and buckets. Air pressure bottles utilize pressurized air to dispel foam, providing a consistent and efficient application for larger areas. Hand-pressing bottles require manual force to create foam, offering portability and ease for smaller cleaning tasks. Buckets provide a traditional method, allowing users to mix and apply foam manually for larger spaces, making them versatile for various cleaning needs. Each type caters to different consumer preferences and applications.</p></p>
