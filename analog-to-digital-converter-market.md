<p><p>The Analog to Digital Converter (ADC) market comprises several types, each suited for specific applications. Display ADCs handle video signals for monitors, ensuring accurate color representation. Delta-sigma ADCs excel in high resolution and noise reduction, often used in audio applications. Pipelined ADCs offer rapid conversion rates, making them ideal for high-speed data acquisition. Dual slope ADCs, known for their accuracy in low-speed applications, are commonly used in digital multimeters. Together, these ADC types cater to diverse technological needs.</p></p>
