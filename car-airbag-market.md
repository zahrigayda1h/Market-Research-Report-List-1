<p><p>The car airbag market comprises various types designed for enhanced passenger safety. Frontal airbags deploy from the steering wheel and dashboard to protect occupants during head-on collisions. Side airbags, located in the vehicle's doors, shield passengers from side impacts. Curtain airbags deploy from above the windows to safeguard heads during rollovers. Knee airbags, positioned under the dashboard, prevent leg injuries during collisions. Additionally, "Other" airbags may include specialized designs, such as those for rear passengers or pedestrian protection.</p></p>
