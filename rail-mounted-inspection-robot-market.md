<p><p>The rail-mounted inspection robot market is divided into two main types: vertical rail-mounted robots and horizontal rail-mounted robots. Vertical rail-mounted robots operate on vertical tracks, enabling inspection in high-rise or complex structures such as bridges and towers. In contrast, horizontal rail-mounted robots travel along horizontal tracks, making them ideal for inspecting pipelines, highways, and flat surfaces. Both types are designed to enhance automation, improve inspection efficiency, and ensure safety in various industrial applications, minimizing manual labor and reducing risks.</p></p>
