<p><strong>The Underground Service Locator Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Active Location</li><li>Passive Location</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Underground Service Locator market can be categorized into two main types: active location and passive location. Active location involves the use of electromagnetic signals to locate underground utilities, providing precise and real-time data for infrastructure management. In contrast, passive location relies on the detection of existing signals from underground utilities, such as those generated by power lines or communication cables. Both methods are essential for ensuring safe excavation and construction, reducing the risk of damage to buried services.</p></p>
