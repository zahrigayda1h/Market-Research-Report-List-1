<p><p>The Automotive Collision Avoidance Systems market consists of four key technologies: Radar, Lidar, Cameras, and Ultrasonic sensors. Radar systems use radio waves to detect objects and measure their speed, providing reliable performance in various weather conditions. Lidar employs laser light to create high-resolution 3D maps of surroundings, enhancing object detection accuracy. Cameras capture visual data for recognizing pedestrians and traffic signals, while Ultrasonic sensors utilize sound waves to detect objects at close range, useful for low-speed maneuvers. Each technology contributes to overall vehicle safety and awareness.</p></p>
