<p><strong>Market Segmentation</strong></p>
<p><strong>The Automated Optical Metrology Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Chemiluminescence Immunoassay (CLIA)</li><li>Enzyme Linked Fluorescent Immunoassay (ELFA)</li><li>Enzyme Linked Immunosorbent Assay (ELISA)</li><li>Radioimmunoassay (RIA)</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Automated Optical Metrology market comprises several key assay types, each employing specific methodologies. Chemiluminescence Immunoassay (CLIA) utilizes light emission for detection, enabling highly sensitive measurements. Enzyme Linked Fluorescent Immunoassay (ELFA) combines enzyme reactions with fluorescent detection for enhanced accuracy. Enzyme Linked Immunosorbent Assay (ELISA) relies on enzymes linked to antibodies for quantifying substances. Radioimmunoassay (RIA) uses radioactively labeled antibodies for precise analysis. Together, these methods facilitate efficient and accurate diagnostics across diverse applications in healthcare and research.</p></p>
