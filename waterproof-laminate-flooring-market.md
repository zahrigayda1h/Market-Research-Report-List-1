<p><p>The waterproof laminate flooring market encompasses two primary types: three-layer and multilayer products. Three-layer laminate consists of a top wear layer, a core layer for durability and moisture resistance, and a backing layer for stability. In contrast, multilayer laminate features additional layers, enhancing structural integrity and moisture protection. Multilayer options often provide better sound insulation and increased resilience against wear, making them suitable for various environments. Both types cater to consumer demands for aesthetic appeal and practical functionality in flooring solutions.</p></p>
