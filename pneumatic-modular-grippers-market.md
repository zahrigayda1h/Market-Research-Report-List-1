<p><ul><li>Parallel Gripper</li><li>Angular Gripper</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The pneumatic modular grippers market is primarily divided into two types: parallel grippers and angular grippers. Parallel grippers operate by moving two fingers in a parallel motion, providing a reliable grip for various shapes and sizes. They are ideal for handling flat objects and are commonly used in assembly lines. Angular grippers, on the other hand, utilize a pivot mechanism that allows the fingers to open and close at an angle, enabling them to grasp irregularly shaped items effectively, making them suitable for diverse applications.</p></p>
