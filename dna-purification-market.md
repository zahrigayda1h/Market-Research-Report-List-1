<p><p>The DNA purification market comprises various methods to isolate DNA effectively. Alcohol purification uses alcohol to precipitate DNA from solutions. Silica gel column-based purification employs silica as a medium to capture DNA selectively. Agarose gel-based purification utilizes gel electrophoresis for separation and extraction. Automated DNA purification leverages technology for high-throughput processing, while chemical-based purification relies on specific reagents for efficient DNA extraction. Each method serves distinct applications in research, clinical diagnostics, and biotechnology.</p></p>
