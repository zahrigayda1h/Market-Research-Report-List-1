<p><strong>The PU Casting Machine Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>High Pressure</li><li>Low Pressure</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The PU casting machine market is primarily divided into high pressure and low pressure segments. High-pressure casting machines inject polyurethane at elevated pressures, resulting in dense, high-strength products suitable for automotive and industrial applications. In contrast, low-pressure machines utilize atmospheric pressure for the casting process, leading to lighter, less dense products ideal for applications like soft foam components. Both types cater to specific needs in the production of polyurethane parts, influencing material properties and application suitability.</p></p>
