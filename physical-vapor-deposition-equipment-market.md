<p><strong>The Physical Vapor Deposition Equipment Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>

<p><p>The Physical Vapor Deposition (PVD) equipment market includes several key types: </p><p>1. **Cathodic Arc Vapor Deposition** utilizes arc discharge to create a vaporized metal, ideal for hard coatings.</p><p>2. **Electron Beam Vapor Deposition** involves heating materials with an electron beam in a vacuum, producing high-purity films.</p><p>3. **Sputter Deposition** ejects material from a target by ion bombardment, allowing for controlled thickness and composition.</p><p>4. **Others** encompass advanced techniques like laser deposition and thermal evaporation, catering to diverse industrial applications.</p></p>
