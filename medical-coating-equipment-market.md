<p><p>The medical coating equipment market includes various types designed for specific applications. Ultrasonic coating equipment utilizes high-frequency sound waves to create a fine mist of coating material, ensuring uniform application on medical devices. Vacuum coating equipment employs a vacuum environment to deposit thin films onto surfaces, enhancing properties like biocompatibility and durability. Other equipment types encompass electrostatic spray systems and dip coating technologies, which cater to diverse coating needs across medical instruments, implants, and protective gear, optimizing performance and safety.</p></p>
